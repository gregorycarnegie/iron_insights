Iron Insights FAQ

What does percentile mean?

Percentile represents the share of comparable lifters your value exceeds in the selected cohort.

Where does the data come from?

The app reads bundled competition aggregates generated from the project pipeline and versioned in the repository.

Why do filters matter?

Changing equipment, tested status, age class, and bodyweight class changes the cohort being compared.

How should I read Trends Over Time charts?

Trend lines are generated from yearly buckets within your exact filter set. They are useful for directional context, not guaranteed year-over-year causal conclusions.

Small cohorts can produce missing years or noisy jumps, so avoid comparing lines across different filter combinations as if they were the same population.
