Iron Insights Methodology

Percentiles are computed from pre-aggregated distributions by selected slice dimensions (sex, equipment, weight class, age class, tested status, lift, and metric).

Interactive percentile output lives in the calculator; this page documents assumptions and caveats.

Method notes

  • Percentiles depend on the selected slice and dataset version.
  • Changing equipment, tested status, age class, or weight class changes the comparison cohort.
  • Trend charts are bucketed by competition year and can be sparse for niche cohorts or older years.
  • Trend lines are only comparable within the same filter set; switching filters changes the underlying cohort.
  • Projection and target outputs are estimates, not guarantees.
